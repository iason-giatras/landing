What is the Dresscode app?

Many people are afraid to buy clothes from the Internet because they might not receive the exact clothe size they are looking for as they are unable to try them on themselves. The Dresscode app allows your phone, tablet and iPad to scan the dimensions of your body, in order to make accurate online clothe purchases that are relevant and appropriate to you.
